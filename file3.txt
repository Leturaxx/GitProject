Tourism is the largest and fastest-growing industry across the world. It is a source of revenue and employment. 
It also gives the opportunity for people to understand the culture, civilization, and religious aspects of a country. 
There are many countries whose main source of revenue is Tourism.

Thomas Cook, the famous British businessman is known as the Father of Tourism, because of his contributions that resulted in the birth of modern tourism, 
beginning with the idea of a “packaged tour.” In 1855, he organized his first tours abroad, and in 1872, he embarked on his first round-the-world tour.
